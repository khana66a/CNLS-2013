Summer School


The summer school (June 3 - June 18, 2013) will be held in Central Department of Mathematics, Tribhuvan University Nepal. Accommodation for the participants is arranged in Kirtipur Hillside Hotel and Resort.

This school will introduce the main mathematical concepts and computational methods for solving nonlinear (systems) of (partial) differential equations.

Lecture Courses:
    Advanced Partial Differential Equations (PDE) - Stefan C. Mancas
    Numerical Methods for Partial Differential Equations (NUM) - Harihar Khanal
    Nonlinear Analysis (NLA) - Dhruba Adhikari

The lectures will be supported by a two hour problem session (PS) each day. Tentative schedule is given below.
