Proceedings and Instructions for Authors



Proceedings of the papers presented at CNLS-2013, Kathmandu (June 18-22, 2013) will be published in a special issue [NPSC-CNLS-2013, 22 (2014)] of the journal Neural, Parallel & Scientific Computations, ISSN: 1061-5369, Dynamic Publishers, USA.

Prospective authors are invited to submit their papers starting from October 15, 2013.


    INSTRUCTIONS FOR AUTHORS:

    The paper should be prepared in LaTex using the specific format. Use the template of a sample paper and the Latex class file provided below.

    NPSC_CNLS_2013_sample.tex
    class file.

    Maximum length of the paper is 24 pages.

    The paper should be submitted through the Easy Chair.

    Deadline to submit the paper is December 31, 2013.
