Special Sessions


  • FORMAT OF THE MEETING
    Program sessions will be initiated by 1-hour invited (plenary) lectures to highlight major accomplishments, trends and technical challenges of nonlinear problems in selected fields of research. Papers selected on the basis of extended abstracts will be presented (30 minutes) in special sessions.
