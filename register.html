Registration


  • Conference registration fee is $500 ($250 for students).
    • The registration fee will cover full accommodation.
    The fee does not cover the banquet dinner and guided tours to important tourist destinations in Kathmandu valley.

  • Summer school registration fee for all attendees is $250.

  • Registration.
    • Registration fees will be collected in cash during the registration period at the conference venue.

  • Travel Support
    A limited travel support is available for recent graduates, and participants from SAARC countries.
    Please write a letter of intent and send CV to cnls2013@erau.edu.

  • Reduced Registration Fees
    For local participants registration fee is 5000 NR.
    For detail contact Dr. Kedar Uprety, Central Department of Mathematics, Tribhuvan University, Kirtipur.
