Abstracts


    INSTRUCTIONS FOR AUTHORS:

    Prospective authors are invited to submit an abstract of no more than one page from January 2, 2013.

    The abstract should include: title of the paper, name, affiliation, and e-mail address.

    Deadline for Abstract Submission: CLOSED

    Notice of Acceptance and Registration: February 15, 2013

    Abstract should be prepared in standard LaTex and should be submitted through Easy Chair.

    Deadline for Registration and Final Program: May 1, 2013

    Late submission may be considered for presentation if there is space available in the program but will not be included in the Book of Abstracts.
